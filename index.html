About Me

In 2013 I opened a law firm with my partner at the time. He was the attorney and I was everything else – human resources, accounting, management, and marketing. I love marketing and it’s been my career for 12 years. What I haven’t liked from the get-go is that I have to hire digital marketing consultants to make my content, SEO and design ideas come to life. For years I’ve wanted to know how to do it all - the back-end and the front-end. So, I am doing it! I am enrolled in the UCSD Extension Coding Boot Camp to become a Full Stack Developer. It’s a very intense six- month course that scares the hell out of me, and I freakin’ love it! Like a friend said to me the other day, “If your dreams don’t scare you, they aren’t big enough.

When I am not coding, I like to run, write, cook, hang out with friends, and see live music. Most of all, I love being a mom to my three kids - ages 7, 11, and 14. They are pretty much the most amazing people on the planet, in my humble opinion.
